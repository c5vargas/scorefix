# ScoreFix — marketing copy (plugin listing & UI)

Use these strings consistently across WordPress.org, landing pages, and in-product microcopy.

## Plugin name

**ScoreFix – Boost Lighthouse & Improve UX**

## Short description (≤150 characters)

Fix the issues hurting your Lighthouse score and conversions in one click. No coding required.

## Long description (sales-oriented)

Your Lighthouse Accessibility score isn’t vanity — it’s a signal that real people may struggle to use your site. **ScoreFix** finds the issues that commonly hurt Lighthouse and business outcomes: missing image descriptions, unnamed links and buttons, and form fields without clear labels.

Run a scan, get a simple **ScoreFix Score (0–100)**, and review a clear list of what to fix — explained in plain language tied to **readability**, **trust**, and **conversions**. When you’re ready, turn on **Apply Fixes** and ScoreFix applies **real changes to your HTML output** (not a fake overlay).

Built for WordPress owners, WooCommerce stores, and small agencies who want results **without code** — and a path to grow into deeper audits as your needs scale.

## Differentiation (one-liners)

- **Real fixes, not overlays** — We improve names and labels in actual markup.
- **Lighthouse + UX + conversions** — Not “checkbox compliance” only.
- **Scan → prioritize → fix** — Not a passive widget.

## Business-oriented issue hints (examples)

- “This issue may reduce conversions…”
- “Fix this to improve readability…”
- “Unclear controls hurt trust and form completion…”

## Limitations (honest)

- Does not guarantee legal WCAG compliance or a perfect Lighthouse score.
- Editorial nuance (best ALT for SEO) may still need a human.
- Some JavaScript-heavy UIs may require manual fixes outside the plugin.
